Prestige Travel was established in 1988 and since has become one of the fastest growing travel companies in North America.

We have developed a reputation for providing quality service, the best value and most of all proven record of reliability and trust from our agents and clients.

Prestige Travel combined with its' cruise operations is one of the largest licensed, bonded and accredited travel companies in the Unites States.
